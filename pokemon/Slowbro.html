Movesets

Water Gun

Level 3

Cooldown 5 seconds

Shoots water forcefully, dealing damage to opposing Pokémon and decreasing their movement speed for a short time when it hits. Holding down the button for this move increases its area of effect and damage.

Slack Off

Level 3

Cooldown 11 seconds

Has the user slack off, continually restoring its HP for a short time. While the user is slacking off, if the user moves, performs a basic attack, uses a move, has a hindrance inflicted on it, or is otherwise interrupted, the HP restoration effect continues at decreased power.

Scald

Level 4

Cooldown 5 seconds

Shoots boiling hot water, dealing damage to opposing Pokémon and creating steam when it hits. Opposing Pokémon inside the steam’s area of effect are left burned and have their movement speed decreased for a short time.

Surf

Level 4

Cooldown 7 seconds

Has the user charge forward on a wave, dealing damage to opposing Pokémon in the area of effect and throwing them. This is followed by two more waves that deal damage to opposing Pokémon in the area of effect and decrease their movement speed for a short time.

Amnesia

Level 6

Cooldown 9.5 seconds

Has the user recover some of its HP using its stored-up lost HP. For a short time after using this move, the user becomes immune to hindrances and gains increased Defense.

Telekinesis

Level 6

Cooldown 7.5 seconds

Makes opposing Pokémon float with psychic power. If used again, pulls the opposing Pokémon toward the user.

Unite Move: Slowbeam

Level 9

Cooldown 112 seconds
