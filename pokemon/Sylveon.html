Mystical Fire

Level 4

Cooldown 7 seconds

Has the user create four small flames while advancing in the designated direction. The flames shoot toward opposing Pokémon one by one, dealing damage and decreasing the opposing Pokémon’s Sp. Atk for a short time when they hit. Each time a flame hits, it reduces the cooldown of this move.

Hyper Voice

Level 4

Cooldown 5.5 seconds

Unleashes an attack of multiple sound waves that deal damage when they hit. The damage they deal increases the farther the target is from the user. Every time a sound wave hits, the next sound wave’s damage increases.

Draining Kiss

Level 6

Cooldown 9 seconds

Has the user blow a kiss at an opposing Pokémon that goes back and forth between the user and the opposing Pokémon multiple times. When the kiss touches the user, it restores its HP. When it hits the opposing Pokémon, it deals damage to it and decreases its movement speed for a short time. If the user and the opposing Pokémon move too far away from each other, the kiss will disappear faster than normal.
